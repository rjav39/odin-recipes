Burger

Description

Don't bother with fast-food burgers with no flavour and instead make your own!

A fast food burger cost 5x more than this burger while this burger is only $2 per burger!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger buns(can be bought in the supermarket)
  • 80/20 ground beef
  • ketchup
  • cheese
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Toast your buns
  2. form the beef into small balls
  3. Heat pan and let it turn hot
  4. Once ripping hot, place the balls and smash with a spatula
  5. DON'T FOR GET TO SEASON YOUR BURGER!!!
  6. Flip once when the burger is ready
  7. Remove your buns and place meat onto buns
  8. Put your favourite condiments and enjoy!
